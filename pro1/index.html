About Us

Barishh is a new addition to Kolkata food destinations, as soon as you step-in, you will feel like you are in some carnival, the place is decorated so nicely takes over you overwhelmingly. Each and every detail has been taken into consideration. From wall art to cutlery, I was quite impressed with the effort taken to give a heavenly experience. They also have an outside seating arrangement, with overall 120 seating capacity. BARishh got a place for you for sure. Now let's talk about the food: First of all the variety in dishes & drinks are astonishing, the menu will take you on a modern Indian food trail by making each of your food palettes crave for more Tomato Rasam - Garlic Rusk Rasam is generally a south indian recipe, but this one is a pimped up version of rasam, where the flavours of tomato kicks in perfectly and you can enjoy it in a cup with a garlic rusk. Smart Adaptation for sure. Should Try! Quinoa & Barkley Salad If you are on a diet and looking to satisfy your taste buds with some delicious dish, then this dish is perfect for you, the quantity is perfect for 2. Very healthy and checks all the boxes for a diet-oriented person. Must try! Avocado Galouti Kebab: The fusion between Avocado and galouti kebab is insane and going to leave you spellbound, the kebab melts in your mouth. The taste of kebab gets a slight flavours of avocado, which makes its flavours very interesting. Beetroot Chop: Crunchy and perfect to munch on if you are enjoying your drinks and shiisha. Lotus stem Magic Masala: I really loved this dish, the crunchy and bit tangy and spicy (depends on your pallet), it was perfect for me. Must Try Desi Buffalo Chicken Wings: Wings gotta make you crave for more, the buffalo wings were cooked perfectly, the wings were tossed well in sauce which made it so delicious, you won't mind grabbing another extra plate. Lamb Boti Tacos: To be straight to the point, this is a must have dish from BARishh kitchen, amazing flavours and perfected. Grilled Butter Chicken with Quinoa & Cauliflower Pilaf: The grilled butter chicken was perfectly cooked and tasted great with Quinoa. I loved the flavour of the gravy. Desserts by BARishh gonna make you drool all over due to its top notch taste.
